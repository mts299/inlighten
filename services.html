Perms

The perm is back!! But in reality it never really left. It's been around forever but the trend comes and goes, but we hope it’s here to stay! Only certain stylists offer this service. Your appointment will come with a mandatory consultation, information and education on how to manage your new permed locks as well as a styling session so get familiar with your new hair texture. These appointments are charged per hour and the shaping and styling session will be included. *Perms are charge per hour*

Hair must be 5” or less which typically would be hair that sits around your ears or just past.

Hair is sitting past your ears and may be as long as your shoulders but not much past.

Hair that is past the shoulders and down your back. If your hair is past your shoulder blades and/or extra thick please let us know so we can accommodate your appointment for you.
